International

Take a trip around the world at Jungle Jim’s! Our International department holds over 50,000 products from over 70 countries to satisfy just about any craving. Travel through Asia and make stops at China, Japan, Korea and Taiwan. Venture over to our Hispanic section for products from South America, Puerto Rico, Mexico and Central America.

You can shop your way through Europe by traveling country by county and aisle by aisle. Get your fill of Middle Eastern cuisine and remember to get great spices in India. International teas and coffees are located here as well as our candy from around the world. Chocolate, fruit, mints, and even bug candies are available!

Asian

Make your way through over 10 Asian countries as you begin your journey around the world. With many aisles dedicated to this part of the world, you’ll find familiar and unfamiliar products as well as ingredients for all your favorite recipes. Find noodles, savory & spicy sauces, traditional candies, teas and more.

With the addition of our frozen and refrigerated section, you’ll have all the ingredients that you need for modern and traditional Asian cooking. If you have a special brand or ingredient for your favorite Asian meal, you’ll find it here!

Indian

Indian cuisine is something to experience, and once you try the food, it’s hard to resist delving further into its mysteries. Here at Jungle Jim’s we carry a world of Indian foods, from traditional items to modern delicacies. Piles of rice bags, famous Indian teas, bulk spices of every imaginable color and scent. If you’re a savvy shopper, you’ll check our Indian section first for bags of spices, nuts, and all types of traditional ingredients used across many cuisines.

For those who don’t have time to cook, we carry many tasty Indian meals, breads, and side dishes that can be heated up in minutes. You can even pick up some Indian soft drinks and sweets at the same time. The more you look, the more you’ll find, in the Indian section at Jungle Jim’s!

Hispanic

Find your way from Brazil to Venezuela and even venture into Mexico as you search for some well-known ingredients and find a few surprises along the way. Looking for Nopales for dinner? How about some guava paste for your dessert? All the different Hispanic countries come together in our International department to give you everything you need. Whether you’re looking to make an authentic Hispanic dish or want to heat up some plantains in the oven, it’s all here and waiting for you at Jungle Jim’s.

View photo gallery

European

No time to take a trip to Spain? Dreaming about touring the castles in Germany? No, we can’t help you get there, but we can help you experience the cuisine of Europe by taking a two hour vacation right here at Jungle Jim’s. Take your time as you stroll through the countries of Europe. Use your imagination and you’ll soon be on a street in Paris, picking out some French jams to try on your scones. Just stop in the country of your choice to take your own two-hour vacation, without all the planning or the cost of a hotel.

Eastern European

Bursting with hardy, rich foods is our Eastern European section. Because this cuisine includes many different countries and cultures, there is a wide range of items from which to choose. We have everything from Hungarian paprika to preserves from Poland. And if you haven’t tried Russian chocolates, now is the time. So stroll through this section of Jungle Jim’s to find hearty foods that will fill your empty stomach and will taste great as well.

View photo gallery

Middle Eastern

Nestled within the International department, you’ll find an array of products from dates to teas and nuts to grains. From pastes & spreads to pastas and breads, you won’t want to miss trekking your cart through our Middle Eastern aisles.

As this food is from one of the oldest cultures in the world, some of our most unique items are found in this section. Grape leaves, olives, and marinated vegetables are packed beautifully in jars, right next to the spices and herbs. We also offer cold and frozen goodies to round out the selection. We wouldn’t want you to linger here all day, but it deserves to be said that our Middle Eastern section can delight all our customers, from the most unsuspecting tourist to the seasoned Jungle Jim’s shopper!

View photo gallery

African

It’s hard to get products from Africa, but if they are available in Ohio, we have them! Our international managers work hard to get all the products you need to make great African meals, and they aren’t stopping with what we have now. Yes, you can get Fufu flour and some great cookies from this area as well as ingredients for traditional African meals.

We also have some authentic brands that you know and trust in this section, but we keep working. So check out our African section the next time you’re in to see our display. Chances are you’ll find something you need!
