Jungle Jim’s Buckeye Beer Bash

No pretension here - just great Ohio beers. We’re celebrating the Buckeye State with a night full of some of the best Ohio breweries we could wrangle, all under one roof. Join us from 7-11pm, we’ll have dozens of beers to sample from our favorite breweries from around the state. There will be live music and we’ll have local food trucks to keep your stomachs full. This is going to be a great Ohio night all around.

Taste of Italia

This july, we're traveling to the shores, hills and fields of Italy! Join us at our Eastgate location, iside Paradise Pavilion, as we take you on a culinary tour through Italy's most savory regions sampling delicious Italian creations from our own International, Seafood and Cheese Departments , as well as items and dishes from some of our most knowledgeable experts and partners. Jungle Jim's Taste of Italia brings the taste and history of Italy to your plates, one delicious sample at a time. Mangia bene!
