Cheers to a Great Festival!

A 10-year Anniversary is a big deal, and we couldn’t be more excited to celebrate 10 years of our International Wine Festival with you. Filled with wine from all over the world, bite size delicacies, charcuterie and more, our 2017 festival will be an intimate, elegant, and exciting two night event.

Lively and vibrant, our International Wine Festival brings education and leisure together as we welcome those new to wine and connoisseurs alike to the Oscar Event Center. After all, we’re pouring and enjoying over 400 wines from more than 90 wineries, and we want everyone to sip, savor and experience these incredible creations in the best way possible.

Much like a bottle of wine, our International Wine Festival is best shared with friends and family. There’s a story to every bottle, and a smile for every sip. Let’s raise a glass together, and toast 10 years of fine wine and even finer company. Salud!

Choose your experience

  • Grand Tasting Experience

    With 90 wineries pouring over 400 wines, the Grand Tasting lets you enjoy delicious food pairings by country, live music, and great company. You’ll also take home a commemorative 2017 Winefest glass and Tasting Guide

  • Connoisseur Experience

    Those looking for a more in-depth experience can upgrade to Connoisseur. Along with all of the benefits of the Grand Tasting, you’ll gain access to our special Connoisseur Only area for a little rest and relaxation, where you’ll indulge in the International “Dinner by the Bite,” exclusive wine sampling, and have full access to the Oscar Bar. Plus an opportunity to participate in our Educational Wine Classes with wine expert Mary Horn.

Buyer’s Club

An integral, beneficial, and unique piece of the Wine Festival experience is our Buyer’s Club program. By signing up before the show, you’re ready to enjoy the festival in the most in-depth way possible. Buyer’s Club members will receive a special, free tasting plate. Once you order your tickets, you’ll have access to sign up for Buyer’s Club. It’s the best possible way to experience the show. Once you sign up, it’s as easy as picking up your Buyer’s Club card at the festival, tasting from hundreds of wines, and ordering the ones you enjoy most. You tell us which location you usually shop, and we’ll make sure your order is ready to pick up the week after the festival.

In order to sign up for Buyer’s Club, you must purchase a ticket to the 2017 International Wine Festival.
Already have your tickets? Great! Sign up for Buyers Club here!

  • Where is the festival?

    Jungle Jim’s International Wine Festival will be held in the elegant Oscar Event Center located above Jungle Jim’s. Please park in the spaces designated for The Oscar Event Center located to the left as you come in the main entrance with the gorilla sign.

    The Oscar Event Center at Jungle Jim’s
    5440 Dixie Highway
    Fairfield, OH 45014
    Top

  • When is the festival?

    Jungle Jim’s International Wine Festival will be held on Friday and Saturday, November 10 and 11, 2017 from 7pm - 10pm.

    Top

  • Will there be a Coat Check?

    Yes, there will be a Coat Check. The Coat Check will be located between the top of the Grand Stairway and the elevator.

    Top

  • Is there an age limit for the festival?

    Yes. You must be 21 to enter the festival. Photo ID is required for entrance.

    Top

  • Can I bring my child?

    No. You must be 21 to enter the festival. Photo ID is required for entrance.

    Top

  • Is there a designated driver ticket?

    Yes. The Non Drinker ticket is $26.63 per person (Price includes tax). Tickets are available online, and in the Beer and Wine Department and at the door.

    Top

  • Where can I purchase tickets?

    All tickets can be purchased, in store and at the door.

    Top

  • Can I purchase tickets at the door?

    Yes. Tickets will be available once you enter The Oscar Event Center.

    Top

  • What is the Grand Tasting?

    The Grand Tasting is where you can taste samples from 400 wines, from 90 different wineries. Also included in the Grand Tasting are food pairings, a commemorative wine glass, a Wine Tasting Guide, live music, water, and coffee.

    Top

  • What do I get for the price of a Connoisseur Ticket?

    As a Connoisseur, you have access to everything that’s included in the Grand Tasting Ticket, plus exclusive wine selections, International Dinner by the Bite (Menu), open bar, and private restrooms; all in your own special Connoisseur Only area.

    Top

  • What do I get for the price of a Grand Tasting Ticket?

    The Grand Tasting ticket includes entrance to the event for one evening, a souvenir tasting glass and a Wine Tasting Guide. In addition to the availability of over 400 wines, you will also have access to food pairings, water, and coffee.

    Top

  • What do I get for the price of a Non drinker Ticket?

    The Non-Drinker Grand Tasting ticket includes entrance to the event for one evening, food pairings, soda, water and coffee.

    Top

  • Where do I get my commemorative wine glass and Wine Tasting Guide?

    You will be given your commemorative wine glass and Wine Tasting Guide upon entrance to the Grand Tasting.

    Top

  • Will there be any food or drink available besides the wine samples?

    Yes. We will have food pairings, samples of food presented by Jungle Jim’s International Market. There will be approximately 6 food pairing booths around the festival. However, samples will not be enough for a meal; they’re meant to help you enjoy the wines.

    The Connoisseur ticket includes "Dinner by the Bite" (Menu)

    Top

  • Will I be able to purchase any of the wines at the Grand Tasting?

    You cannot purchase wines at the Grand Tasting, but you can order your favorite wines with our Buyer’s Club program.

    Top

  • What is Buyer’s Club?

    Buyer’s Club, formerly Purchase Passport, gives you the ability to order your favorite wines from the event.

    Top

  • How does Buyer’s Club work?

    Buyer’s Club is a free service, unique to our wine festival, that allows Buyer’s Club card holders the ability to order wines they’ve tasted for later pick up (at either Jungle Jim’s) just in time for the holidays. After the Festival, we will compile a list of all the wines you requested using your Buyer’s Club card. Your wine will be boxed and ready for purchase the week following the festival. All you need to do is come to the store, pay for it, and take it home. It’s that easy! We will contact you when your wines are ready to be picked up. Wines will be held for 30 days.

    Top

  • Is coffee free?

    Yes. Coffee and water are free.

    Top

  • How much is parking?

    Parking is free.

    Top

  • Where do I park?

    Please park in the area designated for The Oscar Event Center, which is close to the Holden Drive entrance to Jungle Jim’s International Market. You can also access the parking lots by coming in the gorilla entrance (which is the entrance with the digital sign) and turning right. The Oscar Event Center parking is on the right.

    Top

  • Can I take the Monorail to the Event?

    No, the monorail will not be running during this event.

    Top
